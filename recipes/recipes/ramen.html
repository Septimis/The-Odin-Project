Ramen

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp mirin
  • 1 lb ramen noodles
  • 0.25 C bamboo shoots
  • 1 C baby spinach
  • 1 sheet nori
  • 0.25 C carrots (shreeded)
  • 0.25 C scallions (sliced)
  • 3 lb pork shoulder
  • 0.5 C carrots (diced)
  • 0.5 C celery (diced)
  • 1 C onion (diced)
  • 0.5 C ginger (sliced)
  • 0.25 C scallions (sliced)
  • 0.25 C soy sauce
  • 0.25 C garlic
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1.5 gallons chicken stock

Directions

  1. Put all ingredients in hotel pan along with pork shoulder and cook in 350F for 3 hours. Chop meat and reserve liquid for service
  2. Mix soy, mirin, and vinegar and marinate eggs for 20 minutes
  3. Cook the noodles according to the package
  4. Place all garnish in bolw, chopped pork, and ladle over hot broth. Top with egg and scallions
